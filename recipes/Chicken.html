Cocka Doodle Doo Chicken

Description

This will be the most juicy, crunchy, too die for chicken you will ever try.

This recipe is easy to make, perfect for lunch or dinner with some rice on the side.

Ingredients

  • Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Lots of Seasoning

Steps

  1. Portion out chicken
  2. Marinate chicken in milk
  3. Mix dry ingrediants
  4. Coat the chicken with dry ingrediants
  5. Bake it 400 degrees for 35-40 minutes till crispy
  6. Dip it in favorite sauce
  7. ENJOY!!!
