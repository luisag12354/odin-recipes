Cookies

Description

In this scrumptious delicioso recipe has chocolaty chips with just the right amount of crunch and soft touch.

This recipe is perfect for a late night snack while watching your favorite movies.

Ingredients

  • Love and Care
  • Chocholaty Chips
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put everything in the bowl
  2. Make sure you blow 3 kisses
  3. Roll dough into fist size balls
  4. Put into oven for 13.5 minutes at 350 degrees
  5. Eat cookies warm for best taste!
