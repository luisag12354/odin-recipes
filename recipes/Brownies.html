Baked Brownies

Description

These Baked Brownies will make all the ladies fall for you. This Sexy dessert with the contrast of cold and warm will make you die.

These are easy to make and are perfect for whenever someone is coming over.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Brownie mix

Steps

  1. Make Brownie mix as instructed
  2. Add some more eggs, milk, and SUGAR
  3. Put in oven for 30 minutes at 325 degrees
  4. Let Brownies cool off for 5 minutes
  5. Then add Ice cream with powdered sugar
  6. ENJOY!!
